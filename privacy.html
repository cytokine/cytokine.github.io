Privacy Policy

Jaekyung Kim ("Developer") values user privacy and strives to comply with applicable laws. This policy describes how the app SpaceSaver ("Service") handles information.

1. Information We Collect

The Service does not require account registration and does not collect or store personal information. The following non-personal data may be collected to improve the Service:

  • Device information (model name, OS version)
  • App error logs and usage statistics

2. Photo Library Access

The Service requires access to your photo library solely to read original files and save converted versions back to your device. Your photos and videos are processed entirely on-device and are never uploaded to any server.

3. Third-Party Services

The Service may use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Apple (App Store, iCloud, etc.)

4. Children's Privacy

The Service does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such information has been provided, we will delete it immediately.

5. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in laws or service practices. Updates will be posted on this page.
