Frequently Asked Questions

What does SpaceSaver do?
SpaceSaver converts photos and videos in your photo library to the space-efficient HEIF and HEVC formats, significantly reducing file sizes while preserving visual quality.
Will my original photos be deleted?
The app replaces the selected items with converted versions. We strongly recommend backing up your library (e.g., via iCloud or a Mac) before running a bulk conversion.
Does SpaceSaver upload my photos anywhere?
No. All processing happens entirely on your device. Your photos and videos never leave your iPhone.
Why does the app ask for photo library access?
Read and write access to your photo library is required to read the original files and save the converted versions back. The app requests only the permissions needed for conversion.
Are Live Photos supported?
Yes. SpaceSaver converts both the still image and the short video component of a Live Photo.
Which iOS version is required?
SpaceSaver requires iOS 26 or later.
